SGCL applies to any work that:
- Contains, links to, or derives from `/src/ethics/`, `/src/forms/`, or `/src/core/phase_engine.rs`,
- Uses onto-144 profile hashes as behavioral primitives,
- Claims interoperability with ontoCMS Public Hub.

- Deploy autonomous agents based on this code,

You must:
